👥 Flight Crew Consideration

▼

Inexperienced flight crew (OMA 4.1.4):

  • 100 flight hours and 10 sectors within 120 days
  • 150 flight hours and 20 sectors
  • Not more than one inexperienced flight crew shall occupy their operating seat at the same time
ℹ️ Note: New commanders shall operate first 10 sectors as PF unless to accommodate recency requirements

Pilot aged 60 or Older (OMA 4.2.4):

  • Not more than one shall occupy operating seat at the same time

Pilot with Operational Multi-Crew Limitation (OMA 4.2.5):

  • Shall only occupy operating seat if other flight crew member in operating seat:
    • Not subject to OML
    • Not attained age of 60 years
